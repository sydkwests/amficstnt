Everything you need to know about our premium services, booking process, and policies.

Booking & Process

How does the booking process work?

It's simple. Click 'Plan Your Trip' to fill out a quick form. This will automatically open a WhatsApp chat with our team where we will confirm availability, finalize your price, and secure your booking instantly.

How far in advance should I reserve my car?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle model. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests for immediate travel.

Can I book a ride for someone else (guest/family)?

Absolutely. Many of our clients book for family members performing Umrah or business guests. Just provide the passenger's contact details when booking, and we will handle the coordination seamlessly.

The Experience

Do your chauffeurs speak English?

Yes. We understand the importance of clear communication. Our chauffeurs are professional, courteous, and proficient in English to ensure a smooth journey for international travelers.

Do you offer airport Meet & Greet services?

Yes. For airport arrivals, your chauffeur will wait for you in the arrival hall with a name board, assist with your luggage, and escort you directly to your waiting vehicle.

Can we stop for breaks during intercity transfers?

This is your private journey. You are welcome to ask your chauffeur to stop for prayers, restrooms, or refreshments at any time during the trip.

Is smoking allowed in the vehicles?

To maintain our "Immaculate Fleet" standards and ensure the comfort of all guests, Amfics maintains a strict non-smoking policy inside all vehicles.

Payment & Policies

How and when do I pay?

Payment terms are flexible. We typically accept bank transfers or secure payment links finalized via WhatsApp. Cash payments to the driver can also be arranged upon prior agreement.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We monitor your flight status in real-time. We provide a complimentary waiting time of up to 60 minutes after your actual landing time, ensuring your driver is there when you walk out.

Is there a cancellation fee?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations made within 24 hours may be subject to a fee to compensate the assigned driver.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.
